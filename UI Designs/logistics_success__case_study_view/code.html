Supply Chain Bottlenecks & Rising Costs

The client was facing severe supply chain bottlenecks, resulting in unpredictable delivery times and inflated operational costs. Their existing logistics framework struggled with cross-border regulations, leading to goods being stuck in customs for days.

Furthermore, a lack of real-time visibility meant the client couldn't communicate delays to their customers proactively, damaging their brand reputation and customer loyalty scores.

Technological Implementation

We integrated our central API with the client's ERP system, allowing for bi-directional data flow. This enabled the "Smart Route" feature, which calculates optimal routes every 15 minutes based on live satellite data.

Operational Shift

A dedicated Mubraiz fleet manager was assigned to oversee the transition. We moved from a reactive "break-fix" model to a proactive maintenance schedule for all fleet vehicles involved in the trade corridor.
