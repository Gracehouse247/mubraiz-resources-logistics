1. Introduction

verified Key Takeaway

We are committed to protecting your data while managing global logistics operations across the AfCFTA region. Your privacy is at the core of our supply chain excellence.

Welcome to Mubraiz Resources Ltd. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your personal information when you use our websites, software, and services ("Services"). Our goal is to provide transparency regarding our logistics ecosystem operations.

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from using our platform.

2. Data Collection & Usage

analytics Summary

We collect logistics data including shipment locations, weights, and routes to optimize your delivery chain and ensure regulatory compliance.

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company credentials.
  • Logistics Data: Origin/destination addresses, cargo descriptions, and real-time GPS tracking of assets.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and interaction data via tracking cookies.

3. Liability & Logistics Risks

warning Risk Management

While we strive for 100% cargo safety, our liability is limited to standard international freight carrier protocols as defined by regional trade agreements.

Mubraiz Resources Ltd operates as a multi-modal logistics provider. Liability for loss, damage, or delay is governed by the specific bill of lading or transportation contract issued for each individual shipment. For global transit, we adhere to the Hague-Visby Rules and local African transport protocols.
