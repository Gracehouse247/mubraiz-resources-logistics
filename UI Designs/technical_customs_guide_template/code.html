Document Requirements

Standardized documentation is the cornerstone of the AfCFTA framework. Ensure all digital copies are verified through the single-window system.

verified

Certificate of Origin (CoO)

Mandatory
  • Must be issued by the designated competent authority.
  • Electronic signatures are accepted in 38 of 54 signatory states.
  • Validity: 12 months from the date of issue.
lightbulb Pro-Tip

Always ensure the HS Code on your Certificate of Origin exactly matches the one on your Commercial Invoice. A discrepancy of even one digit can result in cargo impoundment.

receipt_long

Simplified Trade Regime (STR)

Optional

Applicable for consignments valued below $2,000 USD to facilitate small-scale cross-border traders.

Customs Process Flow

1

Pre-Shipment Inspection

Verification of goods against quality and safety standards of the destination country.

2

Digital Filing

Upload documentation to the Regional Single Window (RSW) portal for advance manifest processing.

3

Duty Assessment

Customs authorities calculate tariffs based on AfCFTA preferential rates (0% for eligible products).

4

Border Release

Final physical inspection (if required) and generation of the Exit Note.
